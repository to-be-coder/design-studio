# Research move — interviews

Loaded only when the user **explicitly asks** for an interview guide. Never automatic, any round —
interviews are a deliberate act the user triggers. **The user runs the conversations; the skill
never simulates users.** Their notes land in `02 Research/Interviews.md`.

## Generative variant (discovery)
Built from the open unknowns active *this* round — residue-driven, not a generic template.
**Mom Test** format:
- Ask about **past behavior and specifics**, never pitch the idea.
- Treat compliments and hypotheticals as **bad data**.
- Questions stay **open-ended and non-steering** (e.g. "when you pick X, how do you know it's
  right?" — never "would you use a feature that does X?").
- Open with **at least three named learning goals**, each traced to the specific unknown it exists
  to retire.

## Targeted-evaluative variant (pressure-test)
When the trigger is a pressure-test (see `pressure-test.md`) rather than generative discovery, aim
the whole guide at **one load-bearing assumption** instead of the open unknowns broadly: every
question earns its place by whether it could *refute* that assumption. Same Mom Test discipline and
the same three-named-goals rule — the three goals just all ladder to the one assumption under test.
The non-leading craft is the same as the source-side pressure-test.
